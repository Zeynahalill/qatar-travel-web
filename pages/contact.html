Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip through Qatta Travel?
Simply browse our Destinations or Apartments page, choose what you like, and click "Book Now." You'll be guided through a quick, secure checkout process.
Can I cancel or modify my booking?
Yes! You can manage all your bookings from the "My Tickets" section in your profile. Cancellations are free up to 48 hours before your check-in date.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are encrypted and secure.
Do I need a visa to visit Qatar?
Citizens of over 95 countries can enter Qatar visa-free. For others, we recommend checking the official Qatar eVisa portal. We can assist with guidance upon request.
